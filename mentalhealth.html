Discover Types of Mental Health.

Anxiety

Anxiety: Normal response to stress, but excessive worry can interfere with daily life. Anxiety disorders include GAD, panic disorder, social anxiety, and phobias. Symptoms: Racing thoughts, restlessness, muscle tension, difficulty concentrating, sweating, trembling. Treatments: Therapy, medication, lifestyle changes.
View Details

Depression

Depression is a mental health condition characterized by persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and emptiness. It can affect emotions, thoughts, behavior, and physical health. Causes include genetic factors, life events, and brain chemistry. Treatment often involves therapy, medication, and lifestyle changes. It's a serious condition, but with proper support, many people can manage and overcome it.
View Details

Insomnia

Insomnia is a sleep disorder characterized by difficulty falling asleep, staying asleep, or both. It can lead to daytime fatigue, irritability, and difficulty concentrating. Causes include stress, poor sleep habits, mental health disorders, and medical conditions. Treatment may involve therapy, lifestyle changes, and medications.
View Details

OCD

OCD, or obsessive-compulsive disorder, involves recurring thoughts (obsessions) and repetitive behaviors (compulsions). These rituals are performed to alleviate anxiety but can disrupt daily life. Causes include genetics, brain chemistry, and environmental factors. Treatment often combines therapy and medication to manage symptoms.
